# Overall_Expense_Income_Tracker
Overall Expense_Income_Tracker is a powerful all-in-one finance monitoring template designed to give you a complete overview of your income, expenses, bills, and savings in one place.  It automatically summarizes your financial data into clear visuals, helping you make smarter money decisions with confidence.


# General Rules
• Make entries only in plain (unshaded) columns.
• Do not enter data in columns with grey fill — these cells contain formulas and are locked.
• Do not enter data in columns with conditional formatting — these also contain formulas and are locked.
• Expense and Income Tracker entries automatically sum by month and update the Annual Summary sheet.
• The Dashboard automatically pulls data from the Annual Summary to display total income, total expenses, and the annual net balance.


# Benefits of this Tracker

It allows you to:

● Track income and expenses in one place

● Monitor monthly and yearly spending

● View summaries and dashboards automatically

● Organize expenses by category

● Gain clear insight into your overall financial position

● This tool is designed for clarity, simplicity, and control.
